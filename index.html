About

I am a Research Associate with a background in applied economics. My work focuses on applied microeconomics, development, labor markets, and causal inference. I hold an MSc in Applied Economics and Markets from the University of Bologna, after completing a BSc in Economics at the University of Catania. My research experience includes work with Decio Coviello, Nicola Persico, and Denni Tommasi, combining data analysis, survey work, and empirical research implementation.
